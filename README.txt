Control Scheme:

W - Move Forward
S - Move Backwards
A - Move Left
D - Move Right
Left Shift - Sprint, Doubles Speed
Space - Jump
Q - Increase Score
Tab - Pause
Backspace - Restart Level 1
Escape - Exit to Main Menu
Mouse Movement - Look Around in Game, Move Cursor in Menus
Left Mouse Click - Click Menu Buttons
